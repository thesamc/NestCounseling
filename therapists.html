Meet Our Therapists

Megan Ellow, LCSW, Owner

Megan is a licensed clinical social worker in Delaware and Pennsylvania. She graduated with a masters degree in Social Work from Delaware State University. She has worked over 10 years in mental health treatment with children, adults and families. Her private practice specializes in Perinatal Mental Health, Trauma and Anxiety. She has advanced training in Perinatal Mental Health from Postpartum Support International. She is also trained in EMDR, which is a treatment for trauma.

Allison Barbuti, LSW

Allison Barbuti is a Licensed Social Worker in Pennsylvania holding a masters degree from Bryn Mawr College. Allison specializes in working with people managing anxiety, stress, life transitions, parenthood, maternal wellness and trauma. She has training in Attachment, Mindfulness, EFT/Tapping, as well as Meaning Centered psychotherapy and is currently working under the supervision of Megan Ellow toward her Clinical License.
