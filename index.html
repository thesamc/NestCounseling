Nest Counseling

We're Here for the Helpers

Welcome to Nest Counseling, where we specialize in supporting the caregivers and parents who dedicate so much to others. Rooted in compassion and connection, our mission is to help you find balance, healing, and strength in your own journey. Based in Pennsylvania, we proudly serve clients both locally and virtually, ensuring accessible care wherever you are. Our team of warm and skilled therapists creates a space where those who nurture others—moms, caregivers, and helpers—can feel supported and thrive. At Nest Counseling, we're here to help you care for yourself as deeply as you care for those around you.
