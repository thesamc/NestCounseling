Support for Healthcare Workers & Caregivers: Because You Deserve Care, Too

You're the one everyone relies on—the go-to problem solver, the steady hand in a crisis. But being the helper can be heavy, especially when you're constantly putting others first. Whether you're a nurse, doctor, therapist, or caring for a loved one at home, the emotional and physical toll can start to feel like too much. You might find yourself overwhelmed, burned out, or questioning how much longer you can keep this up. You give so much to everyone else, but when was the last time you checked in on you?

Signs You Might Be Struggling:

  • Feeling exhausted, drained, or on the edge of burnout
  • Constant guilt about not doing "enough," even when you're already stretched thin
  • Difficulty switching off from work or caregiving—feeling like you're always on call
  • Anxiety, irritability, or feeling disconnected from those around you
  • Trouble sleeping, racing thoughts, or dreading the next shift or caregiving task
  • Questioning your purpose, career, or feeling resentful about your role

How We Can Help: Putting You Back on Your Priority List

You're used to caring for others, but it's time to turn some of that care inward. Our approach to therapy for healthcare workers and caregivers is all about creating a space where you can be real, let go, and focus on you for a change. Whether you're dealing with burnout, compassion fatigue, or the emotional impact of your work, we're here to support you with practical, down-to-earth strategies that fit your busy life.

Our Approach:

  • Burnout & Stress Management: Learn how to set boundaries, manage stress, and create sustainable self-care practices that actually work. We'll help you build resilience so you can keep doing what you love without losing yourself in the process.
  • Processing Trauma & Emotional Overload: For those dealing with vicarious trauma or tough caregiving moments, we offer a safe space to unpack those heavy experiences. We use EMDR and other trauma-informed approaches to help you heal and move forward.
  • Finding Balance & Self-Compassion: It's okay to not have it all together. We'll work on helping you find a better balance between giving and receiving and reclaiming time and space for yourself.

Outcomes You Can Expect:

  • Feeling more balanced, energized, and less overwhelmed
  • Reduced anxiety, stress, and the constant guilt that comes with caregiving
  • Tools to manage burnout, set healthy boundaries, and protect your mental health
  • Reconnecting with your passion and finding joy in your work and life again
  • A deeper sense of self-compassion and permission to take up space in your own life

You spend so much time caring for others—let us help you take care of yourself. You're worth it.
